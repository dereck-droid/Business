### Industry Context: Product Development

**From Joe's 15 Years Experience:**

**Document-Heavy Industry:**
> "His business is document sensitive and document burdensome. There's so much that has to be documented."

**Statement of Work (SOW):**
- Critical document defining project phases, deliverables, hours
- Both parties know exactly what gets done, when, by phase
- Very rigid process
- Phase-based with stop points to re-quote

**Typical Process:**
1. Start with SOW defining all phases
2. Execute phase
3. Document phase completion (burdensome)
4. Stop at certain point to learn from previous phases
5. Re-quote entirely to the end
6. Continue with next phases

**Why This Matters:**
- Documentation burden between phases might be automation opportunity
- Highly regulated = lots of compliance documentation
- Could be exactly where AI/automation helps

**Players Involved in Product Development:**
- Mechanical engineering
- Electrical engineering
- Industrial design
- UI/UX designers (user interface, graphical user interface)
- Especially for electronic devices

**EE (Electrical Engineering) Note:**
> "That stuff's Martian" - Joe's watched chips being made in Taiwan, seen schematics - very complex
