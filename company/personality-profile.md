**Before Starting Anything New, Ask:**

1. **Can it be completed with AI/automation tools?**
   - YES = Reduces Tenacity demand
   - NO = Will drain energy, proceed cautiously

2. **Does it energize my Wonder and Discernment?**
   - YES = Stay in Genius, sustainable
   - NO = Will burn out quickly

3. **Is there clear revenue within 30-60 days?**
   - In RED ZONE: Must be YES
   - In YELLOW: Should be YES
   - In GREEN: Can be longer-term

4. **Can I hand off execution to systems/tools/partners?**
   - YES = Sustainable, scalable
   - NO = Limited by your Tenacity capacity

5. **Does my Discernment say YES?**
   - Trust your gut
   - If it feels off, it probably is
   - You're rarely wrong when you listen
