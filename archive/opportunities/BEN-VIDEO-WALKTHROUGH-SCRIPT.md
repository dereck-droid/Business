## ðŸŽ™ï¸ DELIVERY TIPS WHILE RECORDING

### Pacing
- **Slow down** - You know it well, Ben doesn't
- **Pause between sections** - Easier to edit, easier to digest
- **Count to 2** after clicking something before speaking

### Narration
- **Always narrate what you're doing:** "I'm clicking on Maria's contact..." "Watch what happens when I drag this card..."
- **Use his team member names:** "Jason sees this..." "Sarah would click here..."
- **Reference his business:** "Maria's pre-foreclosure property..." "Your College Park investors..."

### Energy
- **Match your energy to the impact:** Excited about cool features, calm/clear on important details
- **Smile while talking** - It comes through in your voice
- **Don't apologize** - No "This is a bit messy" or "Sorry, loading slowly"

### Mouse Control
- **Smooth, deliberate movements** - Not jumpy or fast
- **Circle/highlight important elements** - "This number right here..."
- **Pause cursor on key items** - Let Ben's eye catch up
